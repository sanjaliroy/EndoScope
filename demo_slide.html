An AI Scientist Surfacing Research Gaps in Endometriosis
The Problem
  • 190 million women affected by endometriosis worldwide
  • 7–12 year average delay before diagnosis
  • $16M in NIH funding for endometriosis vs. $300M+ for prostate cancer, both affecting ~10% of their populations

  • Women make up 50% of the population but receive only 5% of global R&D funding
  • Only 1 in 5 NIH grant applications succeed — especially hard for PIs in underfunded fields
What EndoScope Does
  • ✦ Surveys real PubMed literature automatically
  • ✦ Identifies the most urgent research gaps
  • ✦ Generates rigorous experiment proposals with full budget breakdowns
  • ✦ Matches proposals to verified funding sources
  • ✦ Verifies all citations against PubMed in real time
